Why Glasses Are Not A Cure

Understanding why traditional methods often fall short in treating myopia.

1. The Root Cause

Myopia is often caused by Ciliary Muscle Lock due to excessive screen time and near-work

2. The Problem with Glasses

Glasses do the focusing FOR the eye. The muscle stops working, gets weaker, and the prescription increases.

3. The Oxylife Solution

We use non-invasive training to relax and strengthen these muscles, restoring natural focal ability
